Secure Login Guide

Pronto Email provides enterprise-grade security with user-friendly access across all your devices. Follow this comprehensive guide to securely access your email account in minutes.

Security First

Always verify you're on the official Pronto Email website (https://app-prontoemail.com) before entering your credentials. Look for the padlock icon in your browser's address bar to ensure a secure HTTPS connection.

Step-by-Step Login Process

01
Navigate to Login Page

Open your preferred browser and visit https://app-prontoemail.com. Bookmark this page for future access to avoid phishing sites.

02
Enter Your Credentials

Type your registered email address and password exactly as created. Enable "Show Password" to verify accuracy if needed.

03
Complete Security Verification

If two-factor authentication (2FA) is enabled, enter the code from your authenticator app or SMS. This adds an extra layer of protection.

04
Access Your Dashboard

Once authenticated, you'll enter your Pronto Email dashboard where you can manage emails, settings, and account preferences.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

  • Forgotten Password: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Follow the reset instructions sent to your recovery email immediately.
  • Incorrect Credentials: Check Caps Lock, ensure correct email format (username@domain.com), and try typing password in a text editor first.
  • Browser Compatibility: Update your browser to the latest version, clear cache/cookies, or try a different browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Edge.
  • Account Lockout: Multiple failed attempts may temporarily lock your account. Wait 15-30 minutes or contact support for assistance.
  • 2FA Not Working: Ensure your authentication device has the correct time/date. Use backup codes or alternative verification method.

Security Best Practices

Always log out of your Pronto Email account when using shared or public computers. Never select "Remember Me" on devices you don't own. Regularly update your password and enable two-factor authentication for maximum security.

Mobile Access Guide

Access Pronto Email seamlessly on your mobile devices:

  1. Mobile Browser: Visit https://app-prontoemail.com in your phone's browser for full-featured access
  2. Email App Setup: Configure your preferred email app using IMAP/POP3 settings (available in account settings)
  3. Push Notifications: Enable browser notifications for instant email alerts on mobile
  4. Biometric Login: Use fingerprint or face recognition on supported devices for quick, secure access
